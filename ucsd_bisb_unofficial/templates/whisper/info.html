The whisper feature is designed to be a place where students can post information anonymously. To that end, you must use an anonymous account to access it, rather than an account attached to your email address.

Here's how it works:

  1. Accept the confidentiality agreement.
  2. Create a new anonymous account. To confirm your identity, an email will be sent to you containing a link with a new account token. Following that link will generate a new anonymous account for you and take you to the activation page.
  3. Activate the new anonymous account by clicking the "activate" button. You will be redirected to the Whisper home page and shown the credentials (username and password) for the account you have just created. This is the only chance you will have to write down the anonymous account credentials, so record them now in a safe place if you want to edit posts that you make later or just have a quicker login process.
  4. Browse posts in the Whisper section and/or make new posts.

Once you have created an anonymous account, the next time you access the Whisper feature you may either log in with your existing credentials or create a new anonymous account. You can create as many anonymous accounts as you like.
