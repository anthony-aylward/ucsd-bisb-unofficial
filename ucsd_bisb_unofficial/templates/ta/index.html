Not all TAships will be listed in this system. - Students enroll in BNFO500 under Dr Ideker - Students need to register for each TAship, even if they have already met the minimum TA requirement - Should not count towards the minimum 12 unit requirement - Submit an EASY request for either 2 or 4 units, include class information, instructor information, paid or unpaid, etc - (https://academicaffairs.ucsd.edu/Modules/Students/PreAuth/) - Attached TA form needs to be completed by both student and faculty, and returned to me before start of TAship - 25% is 2 units, 110 hours - 50% is 4 units, 220 hours See also the [teaching requirement](http://bioinformatics.ucsd.edu/node/25) page of the official website. {% endfilter %}

{{ macros.quote( 'Anyone have any advice on how to find TA positions? I\'m probably quite late for Fall positions but I\'m not sure I even know the best way to find them.', 'OP' ) }}

Another student gave this advice:

  1. Look at what’s being offered in the Fall and email professors directly if you are interested in TAing their course. I think I blasted out like 10 emails one quarter.
  2. Like Bill mentioned, get in touch with the Admins. Personally, I’d reach out to the Bioengineering, CSE, and Biomedical Informatics (Elizabeth Santillanez esantillanez@ucsd.edu).
  3. CSE has a formal TAship application, however it looks like the priority deadline has passed
  4. Open positions can be found here, but I’m not sure how relevant they are
  5. If there is some urgency (e.g. you need to complete your TAship immediately for some reason) talk to Vineet Bafna.
